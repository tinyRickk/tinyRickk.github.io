Research Interests

Focused on bridging theory and practice in quantum information systems

Quantum Key Distribution

Free-space QKD protocols with practical secret key generation rates and information-theoretic security guarantees.

Quantum Cryptography

Secure quantum communication protocols, blind quantum signatures, and quantum-resistant cryptographic schemes.

Experimental Quantum Optics

Implementation of quantum protocols using photonic systems and optical quantum information processing.

Machine Learning

Applying ML techniques to quantum system optimization and quantum advantage exploration.

Blind Quantum Computation

Delegated quantum computation for fully classical clients with quantum-resistant security.

Quantum Information

Theoretical foundations of quantum information processing and quantum advantage.
