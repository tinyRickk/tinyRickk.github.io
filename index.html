I am PhD candidate at University of New South Wales, Syndey; working in the field of Experimental Quantum Communications. My research interests involve Quantum Information and Computation, Quantum Cryptography, Machine Learning, Experimental Quantum Optics, Theoretical Physics. Specifically, my research explores how to implement a free space quantum key distribution (QKD) protocol that features a practical secret key generation rate with the security closest possible to information theorectical security.

Before joining the PhD program at UNSW, I worked in a quantum communications and computations based startup, Qulabs Software (India) Pvt. Ltd., where I worked as a team lead in Blind Quantum Computation (BQC) group for 1.5 yrs. There, my work aimed towards development of a delegated BQC scheme for a fully classical client under the quantum resistant computational security requirement. The first working version of the same was deployed in September, 2023. I had also completed my master from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Roorkee in the year 2022, this is where I first discovered the field of quantum information and got interested in.

When I am not just a boring academic person, I would go to gym, do some calesthenics, go to beach or just randomly drive to some remote locations.
